About Me

Hello there! My name is Katie, and I am a passionate individual who finds solace and joy in the great outdoors. I thrive on the exhilaration of climbing to new heights and exploring breathtaking trails while hiking. Nature is my sanctuary, and it brings me immense peace and inspiration. However, my love for the outdoors is not limited to adventure alone. My family holds a special place in my heart, and spending quality time with them amidst nature is a treasured experience. Their support and love have shaped me into the person I am today.


Professionally, I am a skilled Paintless Dent Repair technician, where I utilize my expertise to restore vehicles to their original condition with precision and care. In addition, I have found fulfillment as a mentor and coach for kids at Kidstrong, sharing my enthusiasm for physical activity and empowering young minds to embrace a healthy lifestyle. I strongly believe in the power of education and constantly seek new knowledge and challenges, both physical and mental. Life is a journey, and I embrace each opportunity to learn, grow, and overcome obstacles, all while cherishing the beauty of the world around me.
